How It Works

Buying:

See an item that tickles your fancy? Click on it, and it will take you to the item’s description page.

If you would like to know more about the item, feel free to leave a question for the seller in the comments section below. If you would like to buy the item, hit the “Buy Now” button, which will pull up a message box.

Once you hit the “Buy” button, you will have claimed the item, and can now message the seller to arrange a time to meet and make the transaction.

Voila!

Selling:

If you would like to post an item to BuyNearMe, hit the “Sell” button at the navigation bar on the top of the screen. This will bring you to a form which you can use to flesh out the details of your listing.

Fill out a title, description, price, category, and other details. Once you are happy with the details of the listing, hit the “Submit” button at the bottom of the form to complete the post.

Now your item is posted to BuyNearMe, and all you have to do is sit back and wait! We will notify you through the site and through e-mail when someone has claimed the item.

Wasn’t that easy?
