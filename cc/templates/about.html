About Us

Buying and selling has never felt so good

From left to right: Seung, Rory, Jessie, Eric, Calvin

BuyNearMe is a student-run social marketplace to help you buy from and sell to your friends and classmates. Use it to sell that furniture that's gathering dust in the corner, or to pick up cheap textbooks without going to the store.

We're making it simpler to get value out of your stuff in a safe and friendly way with people right on campus. Our mission is to make buying and selling easy.

As a new and evolving site, we are very open to hear what you think. Please direct any suggestions, questions, criticisms or death threats to feedback@buynear.me.
