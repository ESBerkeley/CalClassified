About Us

Our Mission: To make buying and selling easy

From left to right: Seung, Rory, Jessie, Eric, Calvin

BuyNearMe is a student-run social marketplace focused on providing an intuitive way for you to sell to your friends and people who share your interests. Use it to sell that furniture that's gathering dust in the corner, or to pick up cheap textbooks without going to the store.

We're making it easy to get rid of your stuff in a safe and friendly way to reach the people closest to you, literally and figuratively.

As a new and evolving site, we are very open to hear what you think. Please direct any suggestions, questions, criticisms or death threats to feedback@buynear.me.
